EMOJI MEMORY GAME INSTRUCTIONS:

Objective: 

The game is played on a grid. The goal is to find and match all pairs of identical emojis hidden behind the cards!

Gameplay: 

- First, click on a card to reveal the emoji. Then, click a second card to try and find its match.

--> If the two emojis match, they stay flipped over and turn a lighter shade of green.

--> If they do not match, they flip back over after it closes the cards and try again!

Winning:

The game continues until all 8 pairs (16 cards total) have been successfully matched. Once the final pair is found, a message will pop up saying you Win!

Resetting: 

Click the "Reset Game" button to shuffle the emojis and start a brand new round.
